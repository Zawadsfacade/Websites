My Favorite Recipe

This dish was something I created on the fly. I was very hungry one afternoon and I had to babysit. So I looked at the fridge and used up random items from the field to make something. It was just bread with a fried egg on top and some leftover pasta rolled over.Coated with Tarmarind sauce. This was the first time I ever cooked. To this day, this ended up being one of the best snacks I just make whenever I am hungry. And today I am going to share the recipe with you all.

Recipe :

These are ingredients you guys need :

  • Butter
  • Roti
  • Egg
  • Tarmarind Sauce
  • Leftover Pasta

Step by step recipe :

  1. Heat up a pan and spread some butter on it
  2. Take your roti and toast it with the butter on both sides
  3. Once the bread is toasted, put a little bit of oil on the same pan
  4. Crack an egg on top of the hot oil and cook for 40 seconds per side
  5. Once the egg is almost done cooking, put some tarmarind sauce on top the egg and flip it and cook again for 20 seconds each side
  6. Now place your egg on top of the toasted roti and heat up your leftover pasta on the same pan
  7. Once the pasta is heated up, place it on top of the egg
  8. Now roll the roti with the egg and pasta altogether
  9. Cut the roti in half and plate it nicely with tarmarind sauce around it and VOILA!
  10. Zawad's Special Egg Wrap is ready!
